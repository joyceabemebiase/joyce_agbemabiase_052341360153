Cardiology Service

At JC HealthCare, our Cardiology service is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for patients with heart and vascular conditions. Our team of experienced cardiologists and healthcare professionals are committed to helping you achieve optimal heart health and reduce your risk of cardiovascular disease.

Our Cardiology service includes:

  • Diagnostic Testing: We offer a range of diagnostic tests, including ECG, echocardiogram, stress tests, and cardiac catheterization, to diagnose and monitor heart conditions.
  • Heart Failure Management: Our cardiologists provide expert care for patients with heart failure, including medication management and lifestyle modifications.
  • Coronary Artery Disease: We offer diagnosis and treatment for coronary artery disease, including angioplasty and stenting.
  • Arrhythmia Management: Our cardiologists provide care for patients with abnormal heart rhythms, including atrial fibrillation and ventricular tachycardia.
  • Vascular Disease: We offer diagnosis and treatment for vascular conditions, including peripheral artery disease and carotid artery disease.

Advanced Treatment Options:

  • Interventional Cardiology: Our cardiologists use minimally invasive procedures, such as angioplasty and stenting, to treat coronary artery disease.
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation: We offer a comprehensive cardiac rehabilitation program to help patients recover from heart surgery or a heart attack.
  • Electrophysiology: Our cardiologists use advanced techniques, such as ablation and pacemaker implantation, to treat arrhythmias.
