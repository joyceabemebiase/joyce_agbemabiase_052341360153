Pediatric Services

At JC HealthCare, our Pediatric Services are designed to provide comprehensive and compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team of experienced pediatricians and healthcare professionals are dedicated to helping your child achieve optimal health and wellness.

Our Pediatric Services include:

  • Well-Child Visits: We offer routine check-ups and health screenings to monitor your child's growth and development.
  • Sick Visits: Our pediatricians provide diagnosis and treatment for acute illnesses, such as colds, flu, and ear infections.
  • Vaccinations: We offer a range of vaccinations to protect your child from serious diseases.
  • Developmental Screenings: Our pediatricians provide developmental screenings to monitor your child's cognitive, social, and emotional development.
  • Chronic Condition Management: We provide care and management for children with chronic conditions, such as asthma, diabetes, and ADHD.

Additional Services:

  • Newborn Care: Our pediatricians provide care and guidance for new parents, including lactation support and newborn screenings.
  • Adolescent Medicine: We offer specialized care for adolescents, including sports physicals, acne treatment, and mental health services.
  • Nutrition Counseling: Our registered dietitians provide personalized nutrition counseling for children with special dietary needs.
