General Health Services

At JC HealthCare, our General Health Services are designed to provide comprehensive and compassionate care for patients of all ages. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals are dedicated to helping you achieve optimal health and wellness.

Our General Health Services include:

  • Primary Care: Our primary care physicians provide routine check-ups, health screenings, and diagnosis and treatment for acute and chronic illnesses.
  • Preventive Care: We offer a range of preventive care services, including vaccinations, health screenings, and health education.
  • Chronic Disease Management: Our healthcare team provides care and management for patients with chronic conditions, such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma.
  • Acute Care: We offer diagnosis and treatment for acute illnesses, such as colds, flu, and minor injuries.
  • Health Education: Our healthcare professionals provide education and guidance on healthy lifestyle choices, disease prevention, and management of chronic conditions.

Additional Services:

Lab Services: We offer on-site laboratory services for convenient and timely testing. Imaging Services: Our imaging services include X-ray, ultrasound, and mammography. Pharmacy Services: We offer on-site pharmacy services for convenient prescription filling and medication management.
