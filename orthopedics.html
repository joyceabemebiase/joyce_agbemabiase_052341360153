Orthopedic Services

At JC HealthCare, our Orthopedic Services are designed to provide comprehensive and compassionate care for patients with musculoskeletal disorders and injuries. Our team of experienced orthopedic surgeons and healthcare professionals are dedicated to helping you achieve optimal mobility and function.

Our Orthopedic Services include:

  • Joint Care: We offer diagnosis and treatment for joint-related conditions, such as osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and joint pain.
  • Sports Medicine: Our orthopedic surgeons provide care and treatment for sports-related injuries, including sprains, strains, and fractures.
  • Spine Care: We offer diagnosis and treatment for spine-related conditions, such as herniated discs, sciatica, and spinal stenosis.
  • Fracture Care: Our orthopedic surgeons provide care and treatment for fractures, including casting, bracing, and surgical intervention.
  • Rehabilitation: We offer physical therapy and rehabilitation services to help you recover from orthopedic injuries and surgeries.

Additional Services:

  • Minimally Invasive Surgery

    • : Our orthopedic surgeons use advanced minimally invasive techniques to reduce recovery time and scarring.
  • Point Replacement
    • : We offer joint replacement surgery for hips, knees, and shoulders.
  • Orthopedic Trauma Care
    • : Our orthopedic surgeons provide emergency care for severe orthopedic injuries.
